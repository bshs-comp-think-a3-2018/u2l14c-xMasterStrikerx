What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is very popular esports game where players of 5v5 go head to head in Summonor's Rift. The map,"Summonor's Rift", is a three lane map where both teams are they're placed in two sides of the map and will try to push through any of the lanes,destory enemy towers that defend the other side of the lane, and inflitrate the enmey base and destory their Nexus to claim victory.If you want to know more or play the game click here to be taken to their offical website.
