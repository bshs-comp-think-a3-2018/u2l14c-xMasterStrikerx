Welcome to the Grid Gaming Club!

What is the Grid Gaming Club?

The gaming club is where you can come in and just hang out with your friends or just to come over do some schoolwork. We also have a gaming team for League of Legends. Think you got what it take to join us in Summonor's Rift? We play other type of games like Yu-Gi-Oh: The Trading Card Game, Magic The Gathering, and many more. If you want more info on the club click here for more.

When and where do the club meet?

The club meets every Monday till 5:00 a.m and meeting at Bryan Station High School in room 201.
